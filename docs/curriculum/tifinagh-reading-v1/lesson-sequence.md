# Tifinagh Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline targets Standard Moroccan Amazigh in Neo-Tifinagh. It is not
final learner-facing copy and requires Amazigh-language review.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `ⴰⵎⴰⵏ`, `ⴰⵣⵓⵍ`, and `ⴰⵎⴰⵣⵉⵖ` as short
  meaningful words.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus optional morphology review.
- Structural family: alphabetic LTR script with visually distinct letters,
  high-payoff identity words, and morphology that should be introduced through
  chunks rather than grammar lectures.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: language-agnostic text
  fields, per-course font classes, script-specific examples, and reviewer notes
  for regional vocabulary choices.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: school labels, cultural-site labels, public signs,
  markets, water and food words, identity text, and everyday labels.
- Known-unit coverage goal: by Lesson 4, learners can read short words using the
  highest-payoff vowels, nasals, z, u, l, gh, and r.
- Known-word coverage goal: by Lesson 8, learners can recognize water, hello,
  Amazigh, bread, river, market, school, and house.
- Deferred high-load material: regional vocabulary variation, full morphology,
  handwriting, historical Tifinagh variants, nonstandard fonts, and copied third
  party examples.

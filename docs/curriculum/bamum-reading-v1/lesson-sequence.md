# Bamum Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline is a review-ready starting point, not final learner-facing
lesson copy. It scopes v1 to modern Bamum block signs unless reviewers choose a
different script-stage boundary.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: recognize modern Bamum signs `ꚠ`, `ꚡ`, and `ꚢ`
  while the first real word anchor is reviewer-selected.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus script-stage extensions.
- Structural family: left-to-right syllabary with modern and historical encoded
  stages, limited corpora, and specialist review needs.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: script-stage metadata,
  syllabary inventory fields, font coverage, reviewer-selected anchors, and
  museum or heritage context notes.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: script identity text, museum and cultural labels,
  dictionary headwords, community literacy materials, and short app-authored
  labels.
- Known-unit coverage goal: establish a small modern-block sign set while
  reviewers select real word anchors.
- Known-anchor coverage goal: replace placeholders with script-name, greeting,
  water, and museum-label anchors before runtime implementation.
- Deferred high-load material: Bamum Supplement historical stages, full syllabary
  inventory, manuscript/paleographic comparison, broad corpus scoring,
  handwriting, and copied examples.

# Thaana Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline targets Dhivehi Thaana reading for practical text. It is not
final learner-facing copy and requires Dhivehi review.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `ފެން`, `މަސް`, and `މަގު` as useful short words.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus optional sign-pair and cultural
  text review.
- Structural family: RTL script with base letters, combining vowel signs, sukun,
  and short word chunks.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: language-agnostic text
  fields, RTL/bidi isolation, combining-mark-aware segmentation, font mark QA,
  and course-specific romanization settings.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: water labels, fish and rice menu words, road labels,
  restroom signs, open and closed signs, school labels, mosque labels, and
  island-travel wayfinding.
- Known-unit coverage goal: by Lesson 4, learners can read short RTL words with
  common base letters, short vowel signs, and sukun.
- Known-word coverage goal: by Lesson 8, learners can recognize water, fish,
  road, rice, Dhivehi, house, coffee, and restroom.
- Deferred high-load material: full alphabet order, dialect detail, advanced
  loanword spelling, numerals, punctuation, handwriting, and copied corpus
  examples.

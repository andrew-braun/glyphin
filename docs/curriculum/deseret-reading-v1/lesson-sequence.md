# Deseret Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline is a review-ready starting point, not final learner-facing
lesson copy. It assumes app-authored Deseret transcriptions reviewed against one
chosen English pronunciation model.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `𐐔𐐯𐑆𐐯𐑉𐐯𐐻` and one practical public-label word.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus historical-context extensions.
- Structural family: left-to-right phonemic alphabet for English with case pairs
  and a historically specific spelling-reform context.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: phonemic transcription
  metadata, case-pair display, font coverage, accessible symbol names, and
  historical-context notes.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: script identity text, simple English labels, historical
  context labels, learner transcription drills, and public-sign examples.
- Known-unit coverage goal: cover core consonants, case pairs, and a compact
  vowel set before accent-sensitive anchors.
- Known-anchor coverage goal: decode at least five short practical words after
  the pronunciation model is selected.
- Deferred high-load material: full Deseret inventory, historical corpus reading,
  multiple accent models, automatic conversion, and copied examples.

# Javanese Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline targets Aksara Jawa reading for practical words. It is not
final learner-facing copy and needs specialist review for spelling and register.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `ꦱꦸꦱꦸ`, `ꦱꦼꦒ`, and `ꦧꦚꦸ` as useful food or
  water words.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus optional pasangan review.
- Structural family: LTR abugida with inherent vowel, sandhangan vowel signs,
  final signs, pangkon, and pasangan forms.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: language-agnostic text
  fields, akshara-aware segmentation, dependent vowel sign rendering, final mark
  display, font shaping QA, and support for stacked forms.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: market signs, food labels, water words, shop signs,
  road and wayfinding labels, cultural-site labels, and primer vocabulary.
- Known-unit coverage goal: by Lesson 4, learners can read four useful words and
  distinguish base letters, u sign, pepet, and final r.
- Known-word coverage goal: by Lesson 8, learners can recognize milk, rice,
  water, market, road, exit, eat, and coffee.
- Deferred high-load material: full aksara inventory, murda and rekan letters,
  numerals, punctuation, handwriting, full pasangan behavior, and copied corpus
  examples.

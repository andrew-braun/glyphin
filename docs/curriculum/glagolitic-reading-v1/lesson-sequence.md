# Glagolitic Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline targets beginner Glagolitic recognition for Old Church
Slavonic liturgical, manuscript, and museum contexts. It needs specialist review
before learner-facing publication.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `ⰰⰸⱏ`, `ⱄⰾⱁⰲⱁ`, and `ⰴⱁⰱⱃⱁ` as reviewed
  Glagolitic words.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 lessons plus optional liturgical-name review.
- Structural family: alphabetic historic script with Old Church Slavonic
  language context, manuscript conventions, yer signs, and liturgical domains.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: historic-language course
  metadata, transliteration fields, manuscript/source notes, sacred-context
  metadata, and Glagolitic font support.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: liturgical texts, manuscript transcriptions,
  alphabet-prayer material, museum labels, and transliteration workbenches.
- Known-unit coverage goal: by Lesson 8, learners recognize core vowels,
  consonants, yer signs, and several high-salience Glagolitic words.
- Known-anchor coverage goal: learners can identify a compact set of alphabetic
  and liturgical words with explicit review caveats.
- Deferred high-load material: manuscript abbreviations, titlo behavior,
  recension differences, exact liturgical passages, grammar, and full alphabet
  order.

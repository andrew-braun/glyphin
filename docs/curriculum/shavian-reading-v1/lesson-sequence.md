# Shavian Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline is a review-ready starting point, not final learner-facing
lesson copy. It assumes app-authored Shavian transcriptions reviewed against a
chosen English pronunciation policy.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `𐑞`, `𐑑𐑨𐑒𐑕𐑰`, and `𐑖𐑱𐑝𐑾𐑯` as phonemic
  Shavian text.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus dialect-policy extensions.
- Structural family: left-to-right phonemic alphabet for English with no case and
  many vowel distinctions.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: phonemic transcription
  metadata, dialect labels, font coverage, accessible symbol names, and optional
  audio hooks.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: script identity text, simple English labels, learner
  transcription drills, public signs, and constructed-script community examples.
- Known-unit coverage goal: cover core stop/fricative consonants and a compact
  vowel set before accent-sensitive anchors.
- Known-anchor coverage goal: decode at least five short practical words after
  the pronunciation model is selected.
- Deferred high-load material: full Shavian inventory, dialect comparison,
  automatic English-to-Shavian conversion, TTS generation, and copied community
  examples.

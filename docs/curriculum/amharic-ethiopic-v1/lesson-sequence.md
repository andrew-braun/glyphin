# Amharic Ethiopic Reading Lesson Sequence

This v1 teaches Amharic Ethiopic fidel through high-payoff words before the full
syllabary chart. The sequence treats each fidel as a readable syllabic unit while
surfacing consonant-family and vowel-order behavior.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `ቡና`, `ሻይ`, and `ውሃ` as cafe and survival
  words.
- Approximate lesson count: 16 core lessons plus late facility review.
- Structural family: Ethiopic fidel syllabary or abugida with consonant families,
  seven vowel orders, visible word spacing, and punctuation distinct from Latin.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: language-agnostic text
  fields, fidel family and order metadata, syllable-level drill display,
  Ethiopic punctuation support, and font rendering checks.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: cafe menus, food signs, market labels, price tags,
  taxi or bus signs, hotel signage, road labels, entrance/exit signs, and public
  facility labels.
- Known-unit goal: by Lesson 4, learners can read short cafe and survival words;
  by Lesson 9, they have seen first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth, and
  seventh order behavior through real words.
- Known-word goal: by Lesson 15, learners can read a compact market, transit,
  hotel, entrance/exit, and facility set with support.
- Deferred high-load material: full fidel chart, labiovelar series, punctuation
  mastery, gemination, morphology, spelling alternations, handwriting, regional
  language comparison, and full grammar.

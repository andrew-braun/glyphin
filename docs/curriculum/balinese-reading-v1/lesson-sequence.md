# Balinese Reading Lesson Sequence

This staged outline targets Balinese-script reading for practical and cultural
words. It is not final learner-facing copy and requires Balinese-script review.

## Course Shape

- First-session reading win: read `ᬩᬮᬶ`, `ᬦᬲᬶ`, and `ᬲᬸᬲᬸ` as short real words.
- Approximate lesson count: 12 core lessons plus optional conjunct and ritual
  text review.
- Structural family: LTR abugida with inherent vowel, dependent vowel signs,
  final signs, adeg-adeg, and script-specific punctuation.
- Required app support beyond the current Thai runtime: language-agnostic text
  fields, akshara-aware segmentation, dependent mark rendering, final sign
  display, font shaping QA, and cultural-context review fields.

## Coverage Notes

- Target-domain samples: food labels, water words, warung and market signs,
  temple and cultural-site labels, roads, language labels, and primer words.
- Known-unit coverage goal: by Lesson 4, learners can read a place name, two food
  words, and water while seeing dependent vowel and final signs.
- Known-word coverage goal: by Lesson 8, learners can recognize Bali, rice,
  milk, water, temple, market, Balinese language, and house.
- Deferred high-load material: full aksara inventory, gantungan depth, ritual
  registers, numerals, punctuation, handwriting, and copied third-party examples.
